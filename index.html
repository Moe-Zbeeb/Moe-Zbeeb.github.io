About Me

I’m an AI researcher and computer engineering student at the American University of Beirut. My interests span geometric & representation learning, neural optimisation, and building efficient generative models for vision, language, and time-series data.

Current Projects

Safety-Guided Self Compression

Developing quantisation and pruning strategies that improve model generalisation while reducing size.

Generative Sequence Models for Malicious Traffic

Converting numerical network flows into symbolic text to synthesize realistic attack patterns.
