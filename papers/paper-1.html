Deep Learning Architectures for Signal Processing

Authors: Mohammad Zbeeb, Alice Johnson, Bob Chen Venue: International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML) 2024 Year: 2024 Keywords: deep learning, signal processing, neural networks, optimization

Abstract

This paper presents a novel approach to signal processing using deep learning architectures. We propose a hybrid model that combines convolutional neural networks with attention mechanisms to achieve state-of-the-art results on benchmark datasets. Our methodology demonstrates significant improvements in both accuracy and computational efficiency compared to traditional approaches.

Introduction

Signal processing is a fundamental task in many applications including speech recognition, audio processing, and sensor data analysis.
