I am Mohammad Zbeeb and this is my blog.

I am a computer and communication engineering student at the American University of Beirut. My work focuses on large language models, reasoning transfer, model merging, and Arabic NLP.

I currently serve as a research assistant with the Image and Video Understanding Lab at KAUST and with Edgebot's post-training team. I also co-instruct courses at AUB on machine learning and AI in medicine.
